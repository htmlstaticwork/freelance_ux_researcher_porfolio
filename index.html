Bridging Data and Design

With over 5 years of experience in the UX field, I help startups and established brands understand their users better. I don't just deliver data; I provide actionable insights that drive product strategy through rigorous methodology and empathetic storytelling.

My approach is grounded in the belief that every design decision should be backed by evidence. I specialize in mixed-methods research, combining qualitative depth with quantitative scale to paint a complete picture of the user experience. Whether it's discovering unmet needs or validating complex workflows, I'm dedicated to making products more human-centric.
